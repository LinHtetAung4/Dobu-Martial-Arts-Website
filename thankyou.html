What's Next?


Before the class, please read our guidelines and rules for students. This will help you prepare for the class and ensure a safe and respectful training environment for everyone.


On the day of the class, please arrive at least 15 minutes before the start time. Wear comfortable workout clothes and bring a water bottle. We will provide the necessary equipment.


During the class, our experienced and certified instructors will guide you through the techniques and principles of martial arts. You'll learn practical self-defense skills, improve your fitness, and challenge yourself mentally and physically.


After the class, please feel free to ask any questions or provide feedback to the instructors. We value your input and strive to improve our services based on your needs and preferences.
