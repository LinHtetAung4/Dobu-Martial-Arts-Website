Dobu Martial Arts, where we are dedicated to helping our customer achieve their full potential through the practice of martial arts. Our school was founded in 1995 by Mauricio Gomez, a fifth-degree black belt with over 30 years of martial arts experience. Since then, we have grown to become one of the premier martial arts schools in the area, with a reputation for excellence in teaching and training.






We are in the business of motivating communities to mobilize and energize one another.

Our Instructors

At Dobu Martial Arts, we are proud to have a team of highly qualified and experienced instructors who are committed to helping our students achieve their goals. Our head instructor, Mauricio Gomez, is a fifth-degree black belt who has trained in multiple martial arts disciplines, including Jiu-jitsu,Muay Thai , Karate and Judo. He brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to our school, and is known for his patient and encouraging teaching style.

Our Programs

We offer a variety of martial arts programs for customers of all ages and skill levels. Our classes are designed to provide a challenging yet supportive learning environment, where students can develop their physical and mental abilities. We offer programs in Muay thai, Karate, Judo, and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, as well as specialized classes for children, women, and seniors.

Our Facilities

Our gym is equipped with a large, state-of-the-art training space that features high-quality mats, punching bags, and other training equipment. We also have a separate area for strength and conditioning training, as well as a comfortable waiting area for parents and family members.

Our Values and Philosophy

At Dobu Martial Arts, we believe that martial arts training is about more than just physical fitness and self-defense. It is also about developing the character and inner strength needed to succeed in all aspects of life. That's why we emphasize the importance of discipline, respect, and self-improvement in all of our classes.

Our Community

We are proud to have a strong and supportive community of students and instructors at Dobu Martial Arts. Our customers come from all walks of life, and range in age from 4 to 84. We believe that martial arts training is for everyone, and we strive to create an inclusive and welcoming environment for all.

Conclusion

We invite you to come and experience Dobu Martial Arts for yourself. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced martial artist, we have a program that will challenge and inspire you. Contact us today to schedule your first class, and take the first step on your martial arts journey!
